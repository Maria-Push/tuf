The Sport You'll Love

Why Play Ultimate?

Ultimate frisbee is more than just a game — it's a fast-paced, inclusive sport built on teamwork and fair play. Whether you're looking for fitness, fun, or friendly competition, ultimate has something for everyone!

Team Spirit

Build strong connections on and off the field.

Speed & Agility

Boost endurance, reflexes, and coordination.

Competition & Fun

Play casually or compete in exciting tournaments.

Play Anywhere

No special gear needed—just a disc and open space.

Spirit of the Game

A sport built on respect, fair play, and self-officiation.

Mind & Growth

Develop strategic thinking, quick decision-making, and resilience that go beyond the game.

How It All Began

What started as a few friends tossing a frisbee has grown into an awesome community of players who love the game and the people around it. Since 2019, we’ve been playing, learning, and having fun — whether at local pickup games or big tournaments. No matter your experience level, there’s always a spot for you on our team!
